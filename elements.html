What do I do in my free time?

Fitness

Physical activity is an essential part of my life. It strengthens my discipline, mental resilience, and long-term consistency — qualities that also influence my professional work and mindset.

Swimming

I have competed in open water swimming events in Mexico, including competitions in Chiapas, Acapulco, and Lake Tequesquitengo. These experiences developed my endurance, strategic thinking, and ability to stay calm under pressure.

Strength Training

I follow structured strength training programs focused on performance, progressive overload, and injury prevention. This practice reinforces my discipline and long-term commitment to improvement.

Running

I run 3–4 times per week and regularly prepare for endurance races. Training for long-distance events strengthens both physical endurance and mental persistence.

Volunteering

I have volunteered internationally, including as an English–Spanish translator for the Tzu Chi Foundation, supporting medical aid missions in underserved communities.

In France, I contributed to MDSE Auvergne, helping support students affected by crisis situations through the distribution of essential supplies and community assistance initiatives.

Creativity

Creativity plays an important role in my life. It began with photography — capturing nature, travel, and everyday moments. You can view my photo portfolio here.

Over time, this interest expanded into digital design, drawing, and painting. Since 2020, I have regularly practiced visual art as a way to explore ideas, experiment, and continuously refine my creative expression.

Travel

Traveling allows me to discover new cultures, perspectives, and ways of thinking. It broadens my worldview and strengthens my adaptability.
